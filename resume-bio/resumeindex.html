Bio

Where do I begin? Well, for starters, my name is Victor Morales and I am a Graphic Design major at Queens College. I am currently a 3D Printing Engineer at Shapeways and a upper Junior majoring in Graphic Design. I was born, raised and currently live in Brooklyn, which I can agree, makes it a little impractical to attend QC. But I am far from a practical person. My switch from majoring in Business to Graphic Design is indicative of that. Had I not attended QC, I never would have come across their wonderful Graphic Design department. It has been a year since I have been enveloped in this major and I find myself more and more interested in learning web design and branding.

I am my biggest design challenge. I have been on a journey to find my calling throughout my entire college career. My initial major was in business and I am thankful everyday to have taken a graphic design class as an elective. Never having done graphic design at all before, this new endeavor was exciting and took much courage to dive into. And in that journey, I have equipped myself with a diverse set of skills and knowledge base. For the last two years, I have been working as a 3D printing engineer at Shapeways Inc. where I developed my professional management skills, communication, researching ability, as well as my design thinking.
